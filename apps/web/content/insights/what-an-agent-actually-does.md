Most explanations of AI agents start with the technology. This one starts with a Tuesday.

At 9:05 a customer emails "where is my order?". The agent reads the email, finds the order in the database, drafts a
reply with the delivery date and tracking link, and stops. A person reads the draft and clicks Approve. That stop is the
whole point: the agent does the digging, a human owns what leaves the building.

The rest of the day looks the same — read, check, draft, ask. No magic, just the tedious middle of the work handled end
to end.
